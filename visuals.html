Never Wet by Rustoleum

I saw this video posted on Facebook and thought it was the most amazing thing ever! A waterproofing system is one thing missing from my life, especially if you’re a messy eater, clumsy, etc.

I can see this product being used in designs for product packaging, clothing designs, posters that need to be weatherproofed, etc. It reminds me of the L.L. Bean button-up shirts my dad used to wear that were wrinkle free and water resistant.

I could use this solution to weatherproof my posters for more hanging options; which, in turn will hopefully reach more people.
